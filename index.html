Memories

Alicante

Alicante is a port city on Spain’s southeastern Costa Blanca, and the capital of the Alicante province.

Valencia

The port city of Valencia lies on Spain’s southeastern coast, where the Turia River meets the Mediterranean Sea.

Rome

Rome, Italy’s capital, is a sprawling, cosmopolitan city with nearly 3,000 years of globally influential art, architecture and culture on display.

Antwerp

Antwerp is a port city on Belgium’s River Scheldt, with history dating to the Middle Ages.

Ghent

Ghent is a port city in northwest Belgium, at the confluence of the Leie and Scheldt rivers.

Bruges

Bruges, the capital of West Flanders in northwest Belgium, is distinguished by its canals, cobbled streets and medieval buildings.

Dinant

Dinant is a city in Belgium’s Walloon Region. It’s on the banks of the Meuse River and backed by steep cliffs.

Brussels

Brussels, officially the Brussels-Capital Region, is a region of Belgium comprising 19 municipalities, including the City of Brussels, which is the capital of Belgium.

Split

Split is the second-largest city of Croatia and the largest city of the region of Dalmatia, with about 300,000 people living in its urban area.

Trogir

Trogir is a town on the central Adriatic coast of Croatia.

Munich

Munich, Bavaria’s capital, is home to centuries-old buildings and numerous museums.

Nuremberg

Nuremberg is the second-largest city of the German federal state of Bavaria after its capital Munich, and its 511,628 inhabitants make it the 14th largest city in Germany.

Ansbach

Ansbach is a city in the German state of Bavaria. It is the capital of the administrative region of Middle Franconia.
